Why Join Us

.

Structure

All the knowledge you need is on the web, but where to start? Our curriculum is focused on exactly what you need to learn, what you can skip, and the best approach.

Mentors

Our mentors are experienced professional developers. Most of them are in leadership positions in software firms and directly involved with hiring for their organizations.

Peers

You'll be working on projects with other sharp and motivated students, able to share questions & lessons learned, while helping each other out.

Hiring Partners

We connect our successful graduates with firms looking to hire junior developers. You'll have the inside track to great developer jobs.

Learn These and More

Not only will you learn Ruby and the Rails framework, but you'll gain valuable experience with some of the most used technologies in the industry. We help you build a great resume.

Course Overview

Our curriculum leads you through a process to become qualified as a junior developer. You'll learn the Ruby on Rails framework and have a basic understanding of JavaScript.

Timeline

Students are expected to work 10-15 hours per week, but with a flexible timeline, you can fit the class schedule to your schedule.

Price

$400 / month and most students are able to finish in 3 months. You receive 6 hours of one-on-one tutoring from our mentors each month.

Guarantee

Within the first 2 weeks if you're not happy with our service, you get your money back. Guaranteed.
