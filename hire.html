What Do Graduates Know?

MSA graduates have experience with some of the most commonly used gems, APIs, and 3rd party services used in the Ruby on Rails community. They will have a junior developer level knowledge of the Rails Framework, Ruby, and Javascript and will have experience working with Pivotal Tracker and Github in a team setting based on BDD and user story acceptance.
