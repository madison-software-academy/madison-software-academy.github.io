Hire a Programmer

Get The Inside Track To New Graduates

By signing up as a hiring partner, you have first access to MSA graduates. You'll be given a report by our mentors on the strengths and weaknesses of each candidate.

By telling us what you are looking for in a potential hire, you can also receive reports on potential good fits with your organization and can request to have specific skills & tools learned by the candidates prior to graduation. Think of MSA as a partner in your recruitment process.

What Do Graduates Know?

MSA graduates have experience with some of the most commonly used gems, APIs, and 3rd party services used in the Ruby on Rails community. They will have a junior developer level knowledge of the Rails Framework, Ruby, and Javascript and will have experience working with Pivotal Tracker and Github in a team setting.
