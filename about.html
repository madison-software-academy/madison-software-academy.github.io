About the Curriculum

We Hire Developers

So we know what skills companies are looking for.

Our founders have been leading teams of developers for over a decade and know what companies are looking for in junior developers. We've specifically tailored our academy to get you to a position of being hired as a junior web developer.

Implementing From Day One

Many other bootcamps talk about learning by doing, but at MSA it is reality. From day one you will be deploying a web application hosted on Heroku. You'll continually add to that application, learning Ruby, RSpec, git, Rails and other important skills firms are looking to hire. We don't even start teaching theory until after you've deployed several web applications.

Mid curriculum is when we start a dive into theory and slow down (just a bit) on the implementation. This is a better approach than other schools (and standard academia) because the theory will make more sense when you have a sense for its application - this is the key to successful learning and the advantage we offer our students.
